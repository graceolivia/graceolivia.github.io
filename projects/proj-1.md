My second main Recurse Center Project was a game made in PyGame. In the game, you play Pip, a mishchzevious cat who leaps and meows her way through a pastel dreamscape. Blueberries give you temporary immortality against the cute but deadly bunny-mice that float through the sky. Land on a star, and you'll shoot into the stratosphere! Get as high as you can without missing a ledge.
